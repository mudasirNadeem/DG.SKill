About DigiSkills.pk


DigiSkills.pk is the largest Training Program in Pakistan offering best Free Online Courses in freelancing Skills with E-Certificates issued by VU and Ignite. Over 3.5 million trainings have been imparted since 2018 to generate skilled workforce in Future of Work and to strengthen digital economy of Pakistan.

The Women Empowerment is a key element of this program, as they benefit to learn and earn sitting in the comfort of their homes.
